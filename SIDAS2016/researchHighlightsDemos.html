Call for Research Highlight and Demos


Authors of recent papers published in highly selective international journals and conferences are invited to contribute in the signature poster sessions providing highlights to their work. Highlight posters provide a wonderful way to raise awareness and visibility of the research, and allow for productive exchange and discussions between authors and attendees; demos from academic and industry groups are welcome. Space is limited, and prior registration and request for presentation/demo are required. Acceptance will be based on quality, topic fitness, and space availability.

Registration to request for research highlight and demo will close on 20 March 2016.
