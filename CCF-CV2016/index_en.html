Important Time

Submission Deadline: May 30, 2016
Notification of Acceptance: June 10, 2016
Main Conference: June 23-26, 2016

CCF-CV2016 welcome researchers to present live demonstrations of their research work and commercial products.

Persons interested in the demo program at CCF-CV2016 should submit a demonstration application form (see below) by May 30, 2016.

Please be advised that the presenters for accepted demos must be registered at the conference. Demos will be selected based on their appropriateness for CCF-CV. If there are more applications than the available space allows, the time of submission of the demo application form will be considered in the selection (with earlier submissions having precedence).

Registration

Please be advised that you must register for the CCF-CV to participate in the demo session. For registration details visit: http://sist.shanghaitech.edu.cn/ccfcv/register.htm

CONTRACTUAL CONSIDERATIONS

Space Selections
The Demo Presenter agrees to accept the space assigned. The Conference reserves the right to modify the designated demo space in the event a change in the original assignment is necessary.

Demos - What is Included?
The Conference will furnish a rectangular table and two chairs, an electric outlet, a poster board and access to the conference internet.

Violations
As a condition for presenting a demo, you must agree to observe all policies.

Insurance
Advise your agent! Insurance on all demo equipment is the responsibility of the demo presenter. You are advised and encouraged to carry insurance to cover your property against damage and loss and public liability insurance for claims of injury to the person and property of others.

Limitation of Liability
Demo presenters agree with CCF-CV that CCF-CV shall not be liable for any damage or liability of any kind or for any damage or injury to persons or property during the term of this agreement, from any cause whatsoever by reason of use, occupation and enjoyment of demo space by demo presenter or any person thereon with the consent of demo presenter and that demo presenter will indemnify and hold harm less CCF-CV from all liability whatsoever, on account of such damage, or injury, whether or not caused by negligence of or breach of an obligation by Exhibitor or its employees or representative.

Catastrophe
In the event of fire, strikes, or other unavoidable occurrence rendering the demo space unfit for use, provisions will be made for the demo elsewhere when possible.
